Key Highlights

Location

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, on the banks of the Yamuna River.

Construction

It was built between 1632 and 1653 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Significance

The Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and is famous for its stunning white marble architecture and intricate carvings.

Taj Mahal Facts

The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most recognized monuments in the world.

It combines elements of Islamic, Persian, Ottoman Turkish, and Indian architectural styles.

It is considered one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
