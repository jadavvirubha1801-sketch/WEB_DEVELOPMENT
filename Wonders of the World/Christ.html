Christ the Redeemer Facts

Christ the Redeemer is one of the most recognized landmarks in the world, located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

The statue was completed in 1931 and is made of reinforced concrete and soapstone.

It was declared one of the New Seven Wonders of the World in 2007.

Christ the Redeemer ✝️

Christ the Redeemer is an iconic statue of Jesus Christ, overlooking the city of Rio de Janeiro from Mount Corcovado. It has become a symbol of Christianity and a cultural icon of Brazil.
