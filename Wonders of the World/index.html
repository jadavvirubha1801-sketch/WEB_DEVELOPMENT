Wonders of the World

  1. Great Wall of China

    Ancient series of walls and fortifications running across northern China — famous for its scale and history.

  2. Petra (Jordan)

    Rock-cut city known for its carved facades and water conduit system — an archaeological marvel in the desert.

  3. Christ the Redeemer (Brazil)

    Massive Art Deco statue of Jesus overlooking Rio de Janeiro from Mount Corcovado.

  4. Machu Picchu (Peru)

    Incan citadel high in the Andes — renowned for its architecture and dramatic mountain setting.

  5. Chichén Itzá (Mexico)

    Large pre-Columbian archaeological site built by the Maya — notable for El Castillo pyramid and astronomical alignments.

  6. Roman Colosseum (Italy)

    Iconic ancient amphitheatre in Rome, symbol of Roman engineering and entertainment culture.

  7. Taj Mahal (India)

    White-marble mausoleum in Agra, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan — celebrated for its beauty and symmetry.
